30146: Deal with Sam when his arm is raised or his knees are bent
Used by the routine at 29912 when bit 5 at 32764 is set (by the routine at 29866, 30070, 30092, 31204 or 31713), indicating that Sam is blowing a fuse, switching a light on or off, raising or lowering a blind, or throwing the hook (and therefore has his arm raised), or is picking up an object (and therefore has his knees bent).
Input
Output
30146 XOR A Clear all of Sam's status flags at 32764
30147 LD (32764),A
30150 JP 60539 Make a sound effect and lower Sam's arm or unbend his knees
