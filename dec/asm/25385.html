25385: Make a gangster chase Sam
Used by the command lists at 64790, 64816, 64846, 64878 and 64902. Makes a gangster start or continue chasing Sam (unless Sam is already being carried or is falling from the roof of a building).

25388 AND 129 Is Sam already being carried, or falling from the roof of a building?
