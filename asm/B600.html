B600: T values (6|y-5)
Used by the routines at E70C and F3A0. T values in this block may be (and in practice are) used by play area tiles at y-coordinates 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 35 and 41. The T values at addresses B6BA, B6BB, B6C3-B6C9, B6E5-B6E8 and B6F6-B6FB are used only by tiles at y-coordinate 41 (which are never displayed), and are therefore effectively unused.
