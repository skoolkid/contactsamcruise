A516: Special location handler routine address
Used by the routine at EBBB. This special location handler routine is invoked when a character moves left from the left edge of the fire escape on the first floor of the apartment building next to no. 19 (see C2EA).
The other special location handler routine addresses can be found at A016, A116, A216, A316, A416 and A616.
