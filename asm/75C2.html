75C2: Deal with Sam when his arm is raised or his knees are bent
Used by the routine at 74D8 when bit 5 at 7FFC is set (by the routine at 74AA, 7576, 758C, 79E4 or 7BE1), indicating that Sam is blowing a fuse, switching a light on or off, raising or lowering a blind, or throwing the hook (and therefore has his arm raised), or is picking up an object (and therefore has his knees bent).
Input
Output
75C2 XOR A Clear all of Sam's status flags at 7FFC
75C3 LD ($7FFC),A
75C6 JP $EC7B Make a sound effect and lower Sam's arm or unbend his knees
