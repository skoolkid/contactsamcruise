6329: Make a gangster chase Sam
Used by the command lists at FD16, FD30, FD4E, FD6E and FD86. Makes a gangster start or continue chasing Sam (unless Sam is already being carried or is falling from the roof of a building).
Input
H Gangster's character number (0xD7-0xE0)
6329 LD A,($7FFC) Collect Sam's status flags from 7FFC
632C AND $81 Is Sam already being carried, or falling from the roof of a building?
632E JP Z,$7A82 Jump if not
6331 RET
