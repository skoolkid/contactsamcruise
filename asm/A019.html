A019: Direction indicators for a character facing left and going left
Used by the routine at EBBB. This list indicates, in descending order of preference, the directions in which a character who is facing left and moving left may go.
A019 DEFB $80 Bit 7 set: left
A01A DEFB $20 Bit 5 set: up/left
A01B DEFB $08 Bit 3 set: down/left
