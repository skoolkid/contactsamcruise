A318: Direction indicators for a character facing right and going up
Used by the routine at EB9B. This list indicates, in descending order of preference, the directions in which a character who is facing right and moving up may go.
A318 DEFB $10 Bit 4 set: up/right
A319 DEFB $20 Bit 5 set: up/left
A31A DEFB $40 Bit 6 set: right
A31B DEFB $04 Bit 2 set: down/right
