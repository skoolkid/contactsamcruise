A418: Direction indicators for a character facing left and going down
Used by the routine at EB9B. This list indicates, in descending order of preference, the directions in which a character who is facing left and moving down may go.
A418 DEFB $08 Bit 3 set: down/left
A419 DEFB $04 Bit 2 set: down/right
A41A DEFB $80 Bit 7 set: left
A41B DEFB $20 Bit 5 set: up/left
