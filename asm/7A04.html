7A04: Send the police after Sam
Used by the routines at 7500 (when Sam finds the dead body in the hotel) and 77A3 (when Sam has called the police).
Input
Output
7A04 LD HL,$7FEB The police flags are stored at 7FEB
7A07 LD (HL),$01 Set bit 0: Sam is wanted by the police
7A09 JP $71A0 Copy Sam's coordinates and location identifier to 7FDB, thus informing the police where they should head for to get Sam
