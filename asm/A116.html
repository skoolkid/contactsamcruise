A116: Special location handler routine address
Used by the routine at EBBB. This special location handler routine is invoked when a character moves right from the top-right edge of the roof of the apartment building next to no. 74 or the apartment building next to no. 19 (see C3F5), or moves left or right from the top-left edge of the roof of no. 17 (see C380).
The other special location handler routine addresses can be found at A016, A216, A316, A416, A516 and A616.
