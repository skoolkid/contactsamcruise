F280: Terminate a command
The main entry point is used by the routines at 7A57, F600, F6DE, F748 and F7BF. Removes the primary command routine address from bytes 8 and 9 of the character's buffer, which has the effect of making the routine at F1FC move immediately to the next command in the command list.
