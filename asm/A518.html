A518: Direction indicators for a character facing right and going down
Used by the routine at EB9B. This list indicates, in descending order of preference, the directions in which a character who is facing right and moving down may go.
A518 DEFB $04 Bit 2 set: down/right
A519 DEFB $08 Bit 3 set: down/left
A51A DEFB $40 Bit 6 set: right
A51B DEFB $10 Bit 4 set: up/right
