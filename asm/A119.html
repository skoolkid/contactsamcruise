A119: Direction indicators for a character facing right and going right
Used by the routine at EBAE. This list indicates, in descending order of preference, the directions in which a character who is facing right and moving right may go.
Input
Output
A119 DEFB $40 Bit 6 set: right
A11A DEFB $10 Bit 4 set: up/right
A11B DEFB $04 Bit 2 set: down/right
