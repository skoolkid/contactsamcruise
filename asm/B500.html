B500: T' values (6|y-4)
Used by the routine at E70C. T' values in this block may be (and in practice are) used by play area tiles at y-coordinates 4, 10, 16, 22, 28, 34 and 40. The T' values at addresses B5BA, B5BB, B5C3-B5C9, B5E5-B5E8 and B5F6-B5FB are used only by tiles at y-coordinate 40 (which are never displayed), and are therefore effectively unused.
