AE00: T values (6|y-1)
Used by the routines at E70C and F3A0. T values in this block may be (and in practice are) used by play area tiles at y-coordinates 1, 7, 13, 19, 25, 31 and 37. The T values at addresses AEDF-AEE4, AEE9-AEF5 and AEFC-AEFF are used only by tiles at y-coordinate 1 (which are never displayed), and are therefore effectively unused.
