72AA: Control a policeman (1)
Used by the command list at FCF4. Makes the policeman begin patrol duty, and chase and pick up Sam when appropriate.
Input
H 0xDE or 0xDF (policeman)
72AA LD L,$08 Change the address of the primary command routine in bytes 0x08 and 0x09 of the policeman's buffer to 72B1
72AC LD (HL),$B1
72AE JP $7360 Send the policeman to a randomly chosen spot on the sidewalk or road
