B700: T' values (6|y-5)
Used by the routine at E70C. T' values in this block may be (and in practice are) used by play area tiles at y-coordinates 5, 11, 17, 23, 29, 35 and 41. The T' values at addresses B7BA, B7BB, B7C3-B7C9, B7E5-B7E8 and B7F6-B7FB are used only by tiles at y-coordinate 41 (which are never displayed), and are therefore effectively unused.
