BE40: Window flags (36<=y<=41)
Used by the routine at F7DA. Since there are no windows between y-coordinates 36 and 41, these window flag bytes are always 0. The window flags for the previous range of y-coordinates (30-35, corresponding to the 1st floor windows) are at BD40.
