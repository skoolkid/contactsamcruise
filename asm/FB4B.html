FB4B: Control a character who has stepped off the roof of a building
The address of this uninterruptible subcommand routine is placed into bytes 0x12 and 0x13 of a character's buffer by the routine at FB52. It controls the character throughout his descent from the roof until he lands.
Input
H Character number (0xD7-0xE5)
FB4B CALL $FAE3 Move the character to the next point in his descent
FB4E RET C Return if the character has not landed yet
FB4F JP $F288 Terminate this uninterruptible subcommand
