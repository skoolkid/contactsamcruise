7A57: Make a gangster walk up or down until Sam arrives
The address of this interruptible subcommand routine is placed into bytes 14 and 15 of a gangster's buffer by the primary command routine at 7A6A. It makes the gangster start or continue walking up or down a few paces unless he spots Sam, in which case the subcommand and current primary command are terminated.
