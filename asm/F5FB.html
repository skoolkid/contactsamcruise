F5FB: Guide a character from one spot on the sidewalk or road to another
The address of this interruptible subcommand routine is placed into bytes 0x0E and 0x0F of a character's buffer by the routine at F600. It guides the character left or right over the sidewalk or road towards his destination.
Input
H Character number (0xD7-0xE5)
Output
F5FB LD BC,$F5EE Point BC at the RET instruction at F5EE
F5FE JR $F5D1 Move the character left or right towards his destination
