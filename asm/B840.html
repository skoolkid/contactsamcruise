B840: Window flags (0<=y<=5)
Used by the routine at F7DA. Since there are no windows between y-coordinates 0 and 5, these window flag bytes are always 0. The window flags for the next range of y-coordinates (6-11, corresponding to the 5th floor windows) are at B940.
