A218: Direction indicators for a character facing left and going up
Used by the routine at EB9B. This list indicates, in descending order of preference, the directions in which a character who is facing left and moving up may go.
A218 DEFB $20 Bit 5 set: up/left
A219 DEFB $10 Bit 4 set: up/right
A21A DEFB $80 Bit 7 set: left
A21B DEFB $08 Bit 3 set: down/left
